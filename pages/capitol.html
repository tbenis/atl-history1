State Capitol: The current state capitol building of Georgia was completed in 1889 on the site of the previous city hall building of Atlanta, and was made a National Historic Landmark in 1974. Built on one of the higher points of the city, the gold domed capitol is a visual landmark in the southern part of the downtown business district, and is the focal point of the state government complex.
