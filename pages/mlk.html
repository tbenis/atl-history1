MLK: was an American Baptist Minister and activist who became the most visible spokesman and leader in the Civil Rights Movement. Born and raised in Atlanta, King Jr participated and lef marches for the right to vote, desegregation, labor rights and other civil rights. In 1964 he won the Noble Peace Prize for combating racial inequality through nonviolent resistance. There are many locations in Atlanta tied to Martin Luther King Jr, such as the The King Birth Home, The King Center, Martin Luther King Jr National Historical Park and the Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. Tomb.
